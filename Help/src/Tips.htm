Tips

Tip Tracker has the ability to track four types of tips: credit card, room charge, cash, and special functions. The tips are entered by server for each date in the pay period, and can be viewed and printed by date or by server.

For payroll purposes, credit card, room charge, and special functions tips are considered charge tips and are payable to the servers that received the tips. Cash tips, on the other hand, have already been paid to the server and are simply a notation of tips that were received but need to be taxed.
