Special Functions Tips

Special Functions tips are the tips paid for group gatherings, such as weddings, banquets, or conference luncheons and dinners.

With special functions tips, the date is ignored when viewing the tips. Instead the tips are filted by function when a special function is selected from the drop down box. However, the tips are reported based on the date, and a date within the pay period is required when entering a special function.

Unlike credit card, room charge, or cash tips, special functions tips must first be assigned a function before the tips can be entered.
