Viewing Tips By Date

Credit card and room charge tips are filtered by date. The date is determined by the current working date for the data file.To change which date's tips are displayed, you must change the working date.

Cash tips are claimed for the entire pay period, so the date assigned to each cash tip is ignored. Cash tips cannot be filtered by date as all cash tips are entered for the same date (the last day of the pay period). Special functions tips are either shown for the entire pay period or filter by function depending on whether a function is selected in the drop down box.
