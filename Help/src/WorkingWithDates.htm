Working with Dates

Dates are required for all tips entered in Tip Tracker. Which date is entered for the tip is determined by the tip type. Credit card and room charge tips are dated based on the current working date of the data file. Cash tips are dated as the last day of the pay period. Special function tips are dated based on the date of the function.
