Cash Tips

The amount in cash tips that each server receives is not necessarily known to anyone but the server. Some servers claim their cash tips and, for those that do not, we estimate the amount for cash tips received. Either way, every server who works during the pay period must claim some amount for cash tips because servers are required to pay income tax on cash tips received.

Cash tips are handled in a different way than credit card, room charge, or special functions tips. Since cash tips are not payable to the server for whom they are being claimed (since they have already been paid), they are not added on a daily basis, but as a total for the entire pay period. Although a date is required by Tip Tracker for any tip being entered, the application automatically assigns the last day of the pay period as the working date for the cash tip. The date is ignored when tips are being viewed, and cash tips will not be reported if the report filters are set to exclude the last day of the pay period.

Adding Cash Tips

To add a cash tip:

  1. Select the Cash tab if it is not already selected.
  2. Enter the server's name.
    3. NOTE: The name is searchable by first or last name.
  3. Enter the tip amount.
  4. Click Add.

Editing Cash Tips

In the event that a tip was added incorrectly, the tip can be edited to change the amount, working date, or type. If you want to change the server the tip belongs to, you must reassign the tip. To edit a cash tip:

  1. Select the Cash tab if it is not already selected.
  2. Select the tip you wish to edit.
  3. Right click then click Edit Selected.
  4. Enter the new amount, working date, and/or tip type.
  5. Click OK.
    6. NOTE: If the tip type is Special Function when you click OK, you will be prompted to select the function.

Reassigning Cash Tips

If you discover that a tip was entered for a particular server, and that the tip actually belongs to a different server, you may reassign the tip to the correct server. To reassign a tip:

  1. Select the Cash tab if it is not already selected.
  2. Select the tip you wish to reassign.
  3. Right click then click Reassign Selected.
  4. Select the server who will receive the tip after reassignment.
  5. Click OK.

Deleting Cash Tips

Sometimes editing and reassigning are not enough and a tip just needs to be deleted. To delete a tip:

  1. Select the Cash tab if it is not already selected.
  2. Select the tip you wish to delete.
  3. Right click then click Delete Selected.
  4. When prompted to confirm the deletion, click Yes to delete the tip or No to cancel.
